Keep the plant alive until Grow = 10

The plant will tell you how to take care of it in the message box, then you click on buttons on the right to add water, sunlight, fertilizer or kill bugs.

Your plant will be happy(say nothing) if data counts are in the range below:
3 < Water < 8
5 < Sunlight
4< Fertilizer < 8
Pest Infection < 5

If data count is not in the range, the plant will give you a message.

You will lose the game if below
Water = 0 or 10
Sunlight = 0
Fertilizer = 0 or 10
Pesticide = 10
